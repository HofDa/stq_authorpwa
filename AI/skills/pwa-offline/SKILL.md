# PWA Offline Skill

## Important rules

- Offline-first
- Avoid blocking startup
- Cache predictably
- Avoid stale critical data
- Prefer deterministic sync behavior

## Constraints

- Field usability is critical
- Low connectivity must be supported
- Keep storage resilient
